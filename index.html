Who Is Harry Music?

The Sound Behind Every Genre

🎶 Harishankar Tiwari

🎛️ Producer & Composer

“I'm Harry Music — real name Harishankar Tiwari, a multi-genre music producer and composer from Bihar, India. I create music that breaks boundaries, blending the raw energy of street sounds with the soul of classical traditions.

From hard-hitting Trap & Drill beats to smooth R&B vibes, from the rhythmic pulse of Afro & Reggae to the rich depth of Indian Classical and Bollywood compositions — my sound covers it all. I don't stick to one lane; I explore every frequency and emotion music has to offer.

Whether you're an upcoming artist searching for your signature sound, or a creative looking for a versatile musical collaborator — I'm here to bring your vision to life, from the studio to the stage.”

Harishankar Tiwari Founder, Producer, Composer
